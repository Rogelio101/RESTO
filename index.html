About us

Haukai Resturant specializes in Maori Kai and is based in Bay of Islands.

It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourist visiting the area.

The owners wants customers to experience Te Ao Maori with the use of Karakai for blessing food and other Maori culture elements.
